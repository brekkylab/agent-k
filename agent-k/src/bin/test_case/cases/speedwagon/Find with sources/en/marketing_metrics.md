# Marketing Metrics

Owner: Marketing
Purpose: funnel and channel performance

These are funnel metrics — visits, leads, conversion rates. They are not revenue.
Percentages and counts here describe demand generation, not money earned.

## Funnel (trailing period)

- Website visits: 240,000
- Marketing-qualified leads: 4,800
- Sales-qualified leads: 1,150
- Closed-won: 410
- Visit → MQL: 2.0%
- MQL → SQL: 24%
- SQL → won: 36%

## Channels

- Organic search: strongest MQL source.
- Content/proof assets: best SQL-to-won rate.
- Paid: efficient for SMB, weak for enterprise.

## What's working

- Proof-led content converts later stages well.
- Webinars drive enterprise pipeline.

## What's not

- Paid social underperforms for enterprise.
- Generic top-of-funnel content brings low-intent leads.

## Plans

- Double down on proof assets.
- Shift paid budget toward retargeting warm accounts.
- Build an integration-effort one-pager for enterprise.

## Notes

- Metrics are blended across segments unless noted.
- Attribution is last-touch; treat as directional.
