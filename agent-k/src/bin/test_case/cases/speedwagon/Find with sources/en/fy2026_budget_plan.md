# KlientCo — FY2026 Budget Plan

Prepared by: Finance
Fiscal year: FY2026 (the plan for this year)
Status: Approved plan (targets, not actuals)

This document holds target numbers for the year ahead. Everything here is a
plan, not a result. These figures must not be cited as last year's revenue —
last year (FY2025) is in the annual report.

## Revenue target (FY2026 plan)

- FY2026 revenue target: $58.0M
- Implied growth over FY2025 actual: ~20%
- Target net new customers: 1,500

The $58.0M is an aspiration, not money earned. It is what we are aiming for this
year, set during planning.

## Targets by segment (FY2026 plan)

- SMB: $22.0M
- Mid-market: $20.0M
- Enterprise: $16.0M

## Assumptions

- SMB churn stabilizes by mid-year.
- Enterprise adds two large logos in H2.
- No pricing change; growth comes from volume and expansion.

## Spending plan

- Headcount investment weighted to product and support.
- Marketing budget flat as a percentage of target revenue.

## Risks to the plan

- If SMB churn does not stabilize, the SMB target is at risk.
- Enterprise timing is the largest single swing factor.

## How to read this

- Targets = plan. Actuals = annual report (FY2025) or flash (current quarter).
- "Last year's revenue" is never a number from this budget document.

## Approval

Approved by the board alongside the FY2025 audited results.
