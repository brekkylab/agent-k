# Operating Expenses Breakdown (FY2025)

Owner: Finance
Purpose: where the money went last year

This file details costs, not revenue. The dollar amounts here are expenses. They
are not income — do not report any number here as last year's revenue.

## Operating expenses (FY2025 actual)

- Total operating expenses: $30.1M
- Research & Development: $11.8M
- Sales & Marketing: $12.4M
- General & Administrative: $5.9M

These sum to the $30.1M operating expense line in the annual report. They are
outflows, the opposite of revenue.

## Cost of goods sold

- COGS (FY2025): $14.0M
- Hosting and infrastructure: $6.1M
- Support delivery: $4.8M
- Third-party data/vendor fees: $3.1M

## Largest line items

- People costs dominate every category.
- Hosting scales with usage; watch it as enterprise grows.
- Vendor fees include the Northstar analytics contract.

## Efficiency notes

- S&M as a share of revenue declined slightly year over year.
- R&D held roughly flat as a share.

## Reminder

Every figure in this document is a cost. For last year's revenue (the income
side), see the FY2025 annual report — its headline total revenue is the number
to cite, not any expense line here.

## Controls

- Expenses reconciled monthly.
- Variances over 5% require a written explanation.
