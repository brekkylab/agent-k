# Product Roadmap (Themes)

Owner: Product
Purpose: directional themes for the year — no firm dates, no feedback data

This roadmap lists what we plan to build. It is not customer feedback and holds
no survey or complaint tally.

## Now

- Proof-led onboarding flow.
- Self-serve password reset.
- Clearer in-app pricing explainer.

## Next

- Slack notifications.
- Scheduled report emails.
- Salesforce integration (enterprise-driven).

## Later

- Mobile improvements.
- More chart types.
- White-label exploration.

## Themes behind the plan

- Reduce onboarding friction (defend SMB).
- Remove top operational ticket drivers.
- Close integration gaps that block deals.

## Principles

- Ship the smallest thing that proves value.
- Prefer reversible bets.
- Measure activation, not just delivery.

## Dependencies

- Onboarding work pairs with the support runbook.
- Integrations depend on engineering capacity.

## Out of scope

- Native mobile rewrite this year.
- Pricing model overhaul.

## Reminder

A roadmap states intentions, not complaints. The survey's most common complaint
is recorded separately in the survey results file; use that for feedback
questions.
