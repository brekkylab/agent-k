# Maplewood Public Library — Member Guide (2026)

## About
Maplewood Public Library opened in 1979 on Cedar Hollow Road. It holds about
412,000 items across four floors. The library mascot is a hedgehog named Quill.

## Hours
The library opens at 10:00 and closes at 21:00 on weekdays, and closes at 17:00
on Saturdays. It is closed every Sunday and on the first Monday of each month.

## Membership
A standard membership allows 20 items to be borrowed at once for up to 21 days.
The late fee is 30 cents per item per day, capped at 12 dollars per item. A lost
item is charged at its replacement cost plus a 5 dollar processing fee.

## Rooms
There are 6 group study rooms on the third floor, bookable for up to 3 hours.
The maker space on the fourth floor has two 3D printers and one laser cutter.

## Annual figures (2025)
The library recorded 318,500 visits in 2025 and circulated 1.02 million items.
Its annual operating budget for 2025 was 4.7 million dollars.
