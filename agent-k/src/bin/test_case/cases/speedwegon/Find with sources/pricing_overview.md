# Pricing Overview

Owner: Product Marketing
Purpose: current published pricing for reference

This describes prices, not revenue. The dollar amounts here are list prices per
plan, not money earned. Do not confuse a plan price with last year's revenue.

## Plans

- Starter: $29 per seat / month
- Growth: $79 per seat / month
- Scale: $149 per seat / month
- Enterprise: custom, contact sales

## What's included

- Starter: dashboard, 1 data source, email support.
- Growth: 5 data sources, exports, priority support.
- Scale: unlimited sources, SSO, audit log.
- Enterprise: SSO, security review, dedicated support, custom contract.

## Discounts

- Annual billing: ~17% off monthly.
- Nonprofit and education: 30% off list.
- Volume discounts negotiated at the Enterprise tier.

## Positioning notes

- Starter exists to remove the "too expensive to try" objection.
- Most SMB land on Growth.
- Scale is the natural step before Enterprise.

## Common questions

- "Is pricing per seat or usage?" — per seat, with usage add-ons on Scale+.
- "Can I switch plans?" — yes, prorated at the next cycle.

## Reminder

These are prices, not financial results. For last year's revenue, see the
FY2025 annual report; for targets, the FY2026 budget. Nothing here is a revenue
figure.

## Change control

Price changes require Product Marketing and Finance sign-off.
