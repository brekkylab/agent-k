- Northstar Analytics: ~$210M

Figures are external estimates and may be a year stale. They describe the
competitive landscape, not KlientCo.

## Positioning notes

- Northstar leads mid-market and is the incumbent in most of our deals.
- Cobalt is closest to us in size and grows integrations-first.
- Beacon competes on support quality at a similar scale.

## Why this matters

- Our own revenue (FY2025, see the annual report) places us between Cobalt and Beacon.
- We punch above our size in SMB but trail in enterprise references.

## Caveats

- Private-company revenue is estimated; treat as directional.
- Pricing models differ, so revenue is not apples-to-apples.
- This file is competitive context, not a KlientCo financial record.
