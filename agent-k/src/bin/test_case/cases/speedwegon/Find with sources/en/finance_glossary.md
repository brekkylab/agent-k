# Finance Glossary

Purpose: shared definitions for financial terms used in our documents.
This file defines terms only. It contains no figures and no revenue numbers.

## Revenue terms

- **Revenue** — recognized income from delivering the product over a period.
- **Bookings** — the value of contracts signed; not yet recognized revenue.
- **ARR** — annual recurring revenue; subscription revenue annualized.
- **Recognized vs deferred** — recognized is earned; deferred is billed-not-earned.

## Margin terms

- **Gross margin** — revenue minus cost of goods sold, as a percentage.
- **Operating margin** — operating income as a percentage of revenue.
- **COGS** — cost of goods sold; the direct cost of delivering the product.

## Period terms

- **Actual** — a number that has happened and is measured.
- **Forecast / target** — a planned number that has not happened yet.
- **Flash** — a quick, preliminary read of a period before close.
- **Audited** — reviewed and signed off by an external auditor.

## Growth terms

- **YoY** — year over year; this period vs the same period last year.
- **QoQ** — quarter over quarter.
- **NRR** — net revenue retention from existing customers.

## How to use these

- "Last year's revenue" means the prior full fiscal year's recognized revenue,
  actual and audited — not a forecast, target, or single quarter.
- When a precise figure is needed, cite the source document that holds the
  actual number, not this glossary.

## Note

This glossary defines meaning, not amounts. It never contains the revenue figure
itself — look that up in the relevant report.
