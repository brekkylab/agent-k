# KlientCo — FY2025 Annual Report (Summary)

Prepared by: Finance
Fiscal year: FY2025 (Jan 1 – Dec 31, 2025)
Status: Final, audited

This is the audited summary for last year, FY2025. Figures here are actuals, not
forecasts. For the current year or next year's plan, see the separate flash and
budget documents — do not mix them with these numbers.

## Headline results

- **FY2025 total revenue: $48.2M** (actual, audited)
- Gross margin: 71%
- Operating margin: 9%
- Net new customers: 1,240

FY2025 total revenue of $48.2M was up 14% from FY2024's $42.3M. This $48.2M is
the full-year actual and is the figure to cite for "last year's revenue."

## Revenue by segment (FY2025 actual)

- SMB: $19.7M
- Mid-market: $16.1M
- Enterprise: $12.4M

The three segments sum to the $48.2M total above.

## Costs

- Cost of goods sold: $14.0M
- Operating expenses: $30.1M

## Cash

- Ending cash balance: $21.5M
- Free cash flow: $3.2M

## Notes for readers
