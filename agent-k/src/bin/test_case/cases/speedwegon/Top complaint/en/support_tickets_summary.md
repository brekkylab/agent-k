issue here is an operational one and differs from the survey's top complaint.

The single largest ticket driver is **"Can't reset password" (27%)** — an
account-access problem, not a product-satisfaction theme.

## Notes

- Password and login issues together are nearly half of all tickets.
- These are operational contacts, not survey sentiment.
- A self-serve password reset could remove the top category entirely.

## Trends

- Password resets spike on Monday mornings.
- Billing questions cluster near renewal dates.
- Integration setup tickets rise after onboarding.

## Actions underway

- Ship self-serve password reset.
- Add an SSO troubleshooting guide.
- Surface billing dates in-app.
